Track Details

Moerangi Road is a rugged, off-the-beaten-track route popular among 4WD enthusiasts. It traverses through remote and challenging terrain, making it ideal for those seeking an adventurous driving experience. The road is known for its steep inclines, tight corners, and often muddy or loose gravel surfaces, especially after rainfall. Due to its rough conditions, Moerangi Road requires a well-equipped 4WD vehicle with good ground clearance and off-road tires. Drivers should also be prepared for potential obstacles like fallen trees, washouts, and narrow passages. It's advisable to check the weather and road conditions beforehand and travel in a group for added safety.
