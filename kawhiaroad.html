Track Details

Kawhia Road, located in the Waikato region of New Zealand, is a scenic yet challenging route that often attracts 4WD enthusiasts. The road leads to the coastal town of Kawhia and passes through hilly and often remote terrain. While parts of the road are sealed, there are sections that can become rough and difficult, especially during or after heavy rainfall. The unsealed sections may feature loose gravel, sharp bends, and steep inclines, requiring careful navigation. A well-maintained 4WD vehicle with good traction is recommended, and drivers should be cautious of changing weather conditions that can quickly alter the road's surface.
