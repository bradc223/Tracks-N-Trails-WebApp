Track Details

Pouto is a small, rural settlement located on the Pouto Peninsula in Northland, New Zealand, at the southern entrance of the Kaipara Harbour. The area is known for its rugged natural beauty, including extensive sand dunes, serene beaches, and dramatic coastal landscapes. Pouto is steeped in maritime history, with many shipwrecks off its coast, particularly around the notorious Pouto Point.

Access to Pouto often involves a 4WD journey, particularly if you plan to explore beyond the main roads and reach more remote areas such as Pouto Point or the Pouto Lighthouse. The 4WD tracks in this region can be challenging, with soft sand, tidal flats, and rugged terrain.
