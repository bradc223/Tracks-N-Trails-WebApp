Track Details

Takahue Saddle Road, located in the Far North District of New Zealand, is a challenging 4WD route known for its rugged terrain and steep inclines. The track crosses the Takahue Saddle, providing a connection between Takahue and Broadwood, but is often only passable by well-equipped 4WD vehicles due to its demanding conditions.

The road is characterized by deep ruts, muddy sections, and rocky surfaces, which can become particularly treacherous after heavy rain.
